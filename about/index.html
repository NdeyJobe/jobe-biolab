About Us

Sunscreen is the one
step no one should skip.

It is the most recommended product in skincare — and the one most small brands never get to offer. We exist to change that.

Why Sunscreen

Daily SPF is the single most effective thing anyone can do for their skin.

Up to 90% of visible skin aging is linked to sun exposure, and broad-spectrum SPF is the most consistent way to prevent it — as well as the front line against skin cancer. Every dermatologist agrees on it. Every routine should end with it.

Yet great sunscreen is hard to make and harder to source. High minimums, compromised formulas, and finishes that fail on deeper skin tones keep clinical-grade SPF locked behind the biggest brands — leaving smaller ones with nothing worth putting their name on.

Our Mission

Give small brands access to sunscreen worth their name.

Our goal is simple: that small and emerging brands get access to high-quality sunscreen they can add to their own line — without the volume minimums or formula compromises that used to make it impossible.

Because when more brands can offer SPF they are proud of, more people wear it. And that is the point: keeping every skin tone protected, every day. That is the standard we hold, so you can hold it too.

About The Founder

Isatou Jobe

Founder, Jobe Biolab LLC

Jobe Biolab was founded on a straightforward observation: emerging brands were being forced into an impossible choice — high minimums, compromised formulas, or a sunscreen finish that simply did not work on deeper skin tones. The problem was never the science. It was the standards.

So we built a private label SPF program held to the standard we would demand for our own brand: clean actives, genuine broad-spectrum protection, and a finish evaluated across every skin tone. Our team brings together clinical research and formulation expertise, and we hold every batch to the same line. Nothing here is a compromise.
