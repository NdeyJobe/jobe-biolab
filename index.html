The Problem We Solved

High minimums. Compromised formulas. A finish that does not work on every skin tone.

Most private label SPF programs force brands to choose between high minimums, compromised formulas, or finishes that fail deeper skin tones. We built ours differently.

Founded by a Harvard-trained biostatistician, Jobe Biolab spent months evaluating mineral SPF formulations against standards for wearability, ingredient integrity, regulatory compliance, and skin-tone compatibility — so emerging brands do not have to.
